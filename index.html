Our Story

Film Making Club (FMaC) is a passionate community of storytellers, creators, and film enthusiasts. We believe in the power of cinema to inspire, educate, and bring people together.

From short films to documentaries, from experimental pieces to narrative features, we explore every aspect of filmmaking while building a supportive creative community.
